About Camille

I'm a UX Designer who loves to craft wonderful solutions that meet business goals.

For 17 years I’ve worked for product companies and agencies, wire-framing, prototyping, and conducting UX research. I create high-fidelity UI screens any time it’s required.

As a deep thinker, I relish the art of problem-solving. My approach involves offering fresh ideas and utilising empathy to craft solutions that resonate with users.

I’ve led visual designers and am well-versed at contributing to design systems.

I enjoy fostering a culture of collaboration and enjoy data-driven decision-making. I thrive in environments where product teams aim to meet a strategic vision through achieving tactical goals.

I'm based in London.
